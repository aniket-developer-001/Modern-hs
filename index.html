Description:

Established in 1949, Modern High School is a government-run institution offering education from Class V to XII. With a thriving student body of over 1200 and a dedicated team of 40 teachers, we are committed to academic excellence and holistic development. Our comprehensive curriculum covers all subjects, fostering critical thinking and intellectual growth. Our experienced teachers are passionate about delivering high-quality education, preparing students for future academic and career success. Our campus boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including infrastructure for all major sports. We believe in the importance of physical education and provide a wide range of sports activities. To support our students, we provide free Books, school bags, uniforms, shoe and cycles, ensuring they have everything they need for their educational journey. We celebrate all major events and festivals, promoting a sense of community and cultural awareness. These celebrations, along with our extensive extracurricular programs, offer students opportunities to explore their interests and develop their talents beyond the classroom. At Modern High School, we are proud of our long-standing tradition of excellence and our commitment to nurturing well-rounded individuals. Join us to be part of a vibrant and supportive learning community.
